Port and Logistics Services

TPC provides berthing, departure, loading, unloading, and in-port shipping and logistics services to bulk and tanker vessels with a capacity of 8,000/10,000 Dwt at the quay parallel to the TPC facility, which is administered and controlled by the Tripoli Port Authority in connection with the TPC operation facility.
