MC1 Bituminous Basecoat

High-performance waterproofing and asphalt coating solution

Product Description

Under the TPC brand, the Libyan Origin Bituminous Primer product is exported from Libya to all over the world.

It is a medium curing cutback asphalt.

A bituminous medium consisting of bitumen and kerosene (as a solvent), it is called an impregnation layer. The ratio of kerosene is 40% and bitumen is 60%.

Benefits of MC1 Bituminous Basecoat

  • It is used to bond the asphalt layer to the gravel base layer or base layer
  • It works to retain moisture in the base layers

Technical Specifications

Property Value
Primary layer spray rate 0.5-1.75 liters/m²
Spray temperature 50-80°C
Minimum allowed spray temperature 15°C
Curing time No less than 48 hours and no more than 7 days after spraying
Kerosene ratio 40%
Bitumen ratio 60%

Application Information

Permitted duration for leaving the layer after spraying:

The MC-1 bituminous basecoat should be left to cure for a period of no less than 48 hours and no more than (7) days after spraying. If the asphalt is not applied after this period, the contractor shall maintain the basecoat until it is covered with a subsequent layer.

The minimum permissible temperature for spraying the MC1 layer is 15°C. The materials most likely to absorb MC1 are A2-4, as they are softer and have a larger surface area, making them more impregnable with MC1.

How to determine the spray rate (liters/m²) or (kg/m²) for the impregnating layer or adhesive layer: A sheet of metal, a 1 x 1 meter tarpaulin sheet, or any other surface with a known area, whatever is available, is used. The spraying vehicle passes over it and is weighed before and after spraying. The spray rate is then determined. If the rate is lower than the specifications, the vehicle may reduce its speed during spraying.
